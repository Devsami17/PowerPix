November 1979 was when the Jackpot chase began. I was in my third year of college at the University of Western Ontario, in London, Ontario Canada and had been studying statistics and programming for 2 years. When I first heard about the Super Lottario, one the first online lotteries in Canada, I was intrigued. How hard could it be to predict a Jackpot? How little I knew then – lol. I remember doing a hand calculation on the top numbers for the latest handful of draws and was sure I was onto something. My best friend, Bob “Hirsty” Hirst and I marched off to the local convenience store to buy a handful of tickets. I don’t recall how many we purchased, remember we were students at the time, but we had high expectations. To say the least we didn’t win the Jackpot, but the passion was ignited.

Fast forward to 2003. I had been toying with theories and models for Lotto 649 for over 20 years at this time, using spreadsheets. I had taken a package at the end of 2002 and had some time to decompress and plan my next career. What a perfect time to buy a bunch of computing power and get to work on cracking the code of Lotto 649! In early spring of 2003, I had developed enough of a model that I shared the results with my future business partner Peter Rose. That lunch was memorable. So much so, we decided to start a company to market these “enhanced” predictions to the general public. From 2003 to 2012, Winners Edge Information Services (WEIS) tracked 257 lotteries across the U.S. and Canada, providing our members with some great picks. We “predicted” about 550 jackpots including some for Powerball and Mega Millions, but we never had enough members to box all of the numbers in order to produce a actual Jackpot winner. We had a notary audit out predictions and still have the documentation today of a Powerball Jackpot we predicted within five (5) draws of getting our “Jackpot Alert” signals from our model.

As it was then, it is today. To have someone win a Jackpot, you require enough members to box all of the combinations. I recently reached out to DraftKings with a proposal for them to offer our predictions to their top players as a benefit of membership. DraftKings reported that they had over 3.1 million MUPs (Monthly Unique Players) in Q2 of 2024. To box every combination of our top ten (10) models, we need to box 3,769,920 combinations. It’s not a requirement, but it’s a lock when you know every combination had been played. If each player plays 5 tickets, this comes out to roughly 750,000 players which is a fraction of their 3.1 Million MUPs.

I’m still pursuing DraftKings and other betting platforms to see if they would like to offer our picks to their members, but in the meantime, we’ve decided to offer them to the public FOR FREE!

That’s right you can play Powerball using 3.7 Million combinations instead of 292 Million. In 2024, Powerball has had eight (8) Jackpot winners to date, Power Pix has correctly predicted three (3) Jackpot winners using 288 Million fewer combinations! More importantly, since 2018 we have corrected predicted over 450, 5 of 5 winning tickets, a period of just over 900 draws. We average a $1 Million, 5 of 5 ticket, every two (2) draws, or six(6) a month, or about 75 per year. Annually, 1 in 10,000 of our 750,000 members is likely to become a Millionaire if they play all 156 draws in a given year! The odds of hitting a 5 of 5 ticket on your own playing Powerball Is almost in in 12 million

Which odds would you rather have 1 in 10,000 or 1 in 12 million?

How can we afford to offer this service at no cost? I’m comfortable, but I’m not independently wealthy. For U.S. players who can purchase their tickets in person or online, we definitely recommend using this approach. For our international players, we are affiliated with an online service, that pays us a commission on ticket purchases which they make on behalf of their members.

I hope this has given you an insight into my history and the reason for creating this site. Please play responsibly. If $10 per draw is too much for you, don’t do it. If you can join a group or create a group of players to make this an affordable and fun experience, then consider trying our recommendations.
